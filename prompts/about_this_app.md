Here is a simple, high-level breakdown of the product:

### 1. What is Ping?
Ping is a lightweight, real-time alert and notification broadcaster designed for small groups, families, and teams. Instead of using heavy, noise-filled chat channels, Ping offers structured, one-way alert channels that users can subscribe to instantly.

### 2. Privacy & Access Model
* There is no public event directory, discoverability feed, or public/private toggle.
* All alert channels (called "Events") are invite-only and can ONLY be discovered, accessed, or joined by scanning a channel's unique QR code or entering its invite code.

### 3. Core Features & User Flows
* **Channel Creation & Management**: Channel owners can create custom channels, specify recurrence intervals (e.g., Once, Daily, Weekly, or Custom), and set default notification messages and urgency levels.
* **Subscription (Joining)**: Subscribers join a channel instantly by scanning a physical or digital QR code. They can view a historical feed of all past alerts on their unified dashboard.
* **Structured Alert Triggering (Pings)**: Channel creators can instantly trigger alerts to all joined subscribers using predefined templates or manual overrides. 
* **Visual Urgency Levels**: Alerts have distinct priority ratings (e.g., Regular, Urgent, Critical) that change how they visually display to the receiver, ensuring high-priority information cuts through the digital noise.
* **Quick Trigger Widgets**: A dedicated action screen allows creators to fire off common alerts with a single tap, without navigating complex menus.
